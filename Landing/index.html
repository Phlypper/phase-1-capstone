Learn VoiceOver

We can teach users the ins-and-outs of Mac’s built-in screen reader, “VoiceOver”.

Learn JAWS

We can teach users how utilize “JAWS (Job Access With Speech)”, a screen reader designed to work with Windows and is developed by Freedom Scientific.

NVDA

We can teach users all about “NVDA (Non-Visual Desktop Access)”, a free open source program designed to also work with Windows-based machines.

About Us

We are a passionate team dedicated to delivering high-quality services. With years of experience in the industry of accessibility, we specialize in teaching users how much they can accomplish in the world of computing with the use of screen readers. We are a group of knowledgeable and friendly people who are dedicated to helping you.

Founded in 2023, our company has helped numerous individuals and businesses achieve their goals without the hindrance of low vision or blindness.
